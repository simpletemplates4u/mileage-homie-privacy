Mileage Homie — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Mileage Homie does not collect your data. The app is an on-device IRS mileage tracker. Your information stays on your iPhone and is never sent to us or any third party.

On your device by default

  • Your locations, photos, odometer readings, notes, and trips are stored only on your device.
  • iCloud sync is off — nothing is uploaded to any cloud.
  • No ads, no tracking, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

The only ways data leaves your device

Data leaves your phone only when you deliberately choose one of these actions:

  • A PDF mileage log you generate and share.
  • A QuickBooks CSV/JSON file you generate and share (business mileage figures only — no GPS coordinates, no photos).
  • A support email you compose and send from your own mail app. Its contents are diagnostics only (app version, iOS version, device model) — never your trips, locations, or photos.

Each of these is a deliberate action you take. Nothing is sent automatically or in the background.

Location

Location is used only after you explicitly start tracking a trip, to measure the distance you drive. It is processed on your device and is not collected by us. Map lookups (directions and place search) are handled by Apple's MapKit.
